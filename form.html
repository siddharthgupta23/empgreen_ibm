FAQs

What is renewable energy?

Renewable energy comes from sources that can be replenished naturally, such as solar, wind, hydro, and geothermal energy.

Why is renewable energy important?

Renewable energy is crucial for reducing greenhouse gas emissions, decreasing pollution, and providing a sustainable energy source for future generations.

How can I switch to renewable energy?

You can switch to renewable energy by installing solar panels, subscribing to a green energy plan from your utility provider, or using renewable energy certificates.
