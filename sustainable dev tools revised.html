8 Sustainability Tools

1. The 2030 Carbon Calculator

The 2030 Calculator is an online calculator that can be used to assess the footprint of your products and then make informed decisions around the design. They explain "the tool enables users to create product value chains by mapping the included materials, processes, and transports by using a combination of company specific activity data and secondary data."

2. FOOTPRINTCALC

The FOOTPRINTCAL tool is a free online product footprint calculator for assessing the environmental impact of products you do need a basic LCA understanding to carry out the assessment.

3. Good.Lab Product Footprint Calculator

This Good.Lab tool is a fee-based online assessment tool. It offers product emissions, footprint and life cycle data and appears to also offer ESG and other environmental assessment and reporting tools.

4. The Circular Design Strategy Wheel

The Circular Design Strategy Wheel is a beautiful open-source collection of systemic design strategies to accelerate the circular economy. I love this tool!

5. Sustainable Design Handbook

The Sustainable Design Handbook is a sustainability guide for anyone involved in the design and production of physical things. It includes a range of industry-specific case studies and design principles.

6. The European Union's Circular Design Tools

A series of practical tools, the European Union’s Circular Design Tools are used by designers and business leaders to establish and advance circular and sustainable design.

7. Quick Guide to Sustainable Design Strategies

Quick Guide to Sustainable Design Strategies is a quick guide that I made to show the suite of sustainable design strategies that can be applied to any product design to elevate its environmental performance.

8. Biomimicry DesignLens

Biomimicry DesignLens shares that life’s principles are design lessons from nature, based on the recognition that life on Earth is interconnected and interdependent. This helps to provide a new perspective to approach design.
